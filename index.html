Rules of the game

There are 5 options to choose from in the game. The game objective is simple, to beat the computer in game of chance. In the list below you can see the different hands. what they are called and who beats who.

  • The clenched fist symbolizes - ROCK

    • Beats: SCISSORS & LIZARD

  • The open palm symbolizes - PAPER

    • Beats: ROCK & SPOCK

  • The hand showing a peace sign symbolizes - SCISSORS

    • Beats: PAPER & LIZARD

  • The hand pinching symbolizes - LIZARD

    • Beats: PAPER & SPOCK

  • The last hand symbolizes - SPOCK

    • Beats: ROCK & SCISSORS
